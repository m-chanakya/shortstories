There was a man named John Smith. He came to this country many years ago, when there were woods, wild beasts and Indians around everywhere. One day, Smith was in the woods and the Indians found him. They took him to their King who ordered his death. They put his head down on a stone. Some Indians raised their clubs to smash his head, when a little Indian girl, the daughter of the King, Pocahontas rushed in. She threw herself betwen Smith and the men with the clubs and said told them to spare his life as he had done them no harm. The King spoke to the warriors and they released him. They escorted him home. After that Pocahontas was a friend of white men, and she did many things to help them.
