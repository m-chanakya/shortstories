William Jones, a captain of a small boat, found a glass bottle with a piece of paper in it. It said there was a treasure on board a ship that was sinking in 1864 and enclosed the course the ship took. Jones told one of his crew that it would pay to go. So they chartered a boat and set sail. In four weeks, they reached. The divers went down and came up with a bottle. The bottle had another note saying the this all was a joke and they will find nothing. It also said that they will find $25 to defray the expenses in a box near where they found the bottle. Jones was angry, and told the diver to get the box. The narrator thinks they will never follow a mysterious bottle again.
