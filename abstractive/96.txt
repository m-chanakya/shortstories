The writer of this story collects things from time to time. He started with stamps when he got a letter from a friend with a three-cornered stamp. He tried to devote his life to it. After three days he stopped. He used to brag about it, but he grew tired of lying. Then he tried collecting coins. He got a few good ones. It collection lasted most of the winter, till one day he spent them all. He tried fossils, but only got two in two years. He tried to collect weapons after a friend showed him his. He ended up presenting his collection to a lady as a present, and she thought the gift was too much. The relationship ceased. He recommends that a beginner starts with coins. He's moved on to bills now.
