An A.D.T. Kid carrying a Death Message marked «Rush» stopped in front of a Show Window containing a Picture of James J. Jeffries and began to weep bitterly.
A kind-hearted Suburbanite happened to be passing along on his Way to the 5:42 Train. «Why do you weep?» he asked, peering at the Boy through his concavo-convex Nose Glasses.
«Oh, gee! I was just Thinking,» replied the Urchin, brokenly.
